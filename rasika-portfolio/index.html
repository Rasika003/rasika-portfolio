HR Projects

01

Attrition Early Warning Dashboard

Problem

HR had no proactive signal for attrition risk, reacting only post-resignation.

Solution & Impact

Built a predictive risk model using HRIS data. Designed a weighted Excel scoring model integrating tenure, performance ratings, absenteeism, and salary bands. Built a red-amber-green department heatmap, correlated eNPS dips with risk scores to identify engagement-driven flight risks. Delivered a monthly attrition report template and a three-tier retention action playbook giving HR a 60–90 day advance warning on potential exits.

Excel HRIS Data eNPS Heatmapping
02

Passive Candidate Intelligence Pipeline

Problem

Job-posting-only recruitment consistently missed passive talent pools.

Solution & Impact

Built an AI-assisted sourcing and structured assessment workflow. Used Juicebox and SeekOut to source passive candidates. Built a weighted Excel scoring matrix on must-have skills, culture fit, and career trajectory. Created a Greenhouse Interview Kit with structured scorecards, templated candidate status emails, and documented the workflow as a complete SOP. Produced a reusable sourcing-to-assessment pipeline reducing unstructured hiring decisions.

Juicebox SeekOut Greenhouse ATS Excel
03

Zero-Gap Onboarding Tracker with IT-HR Integration

Problem

Onboarding handoffs between HR, IT, Finance, and managers created costly delays and compliance gaps.

Solution & Impact

Audited workflows in Rippling and BambooHR tracing device provisioning, payroll setup, and system access triggers. Built an Excel master tracker with conditional formatting to flag delayed tasks in real time. Produced a before-and-after PowerPoint deck with time-saved metrics and cost-of-bad-onboarding calculation. Delivered a mapped IT-HR integration SOP and a live tracker eliminating manual follow-up effort.

Rippling BambooHR Excel PowerPoint
04

People Analytics CHRO Report: Headcount Cost vs. Performance

Problem

Leadership lacked a structured view linking payroll cost to employee performance tiers.

Solution & Impact

Analyzed 50-employee dataset in Excel pivot tables across department, salary band, performance rating, tenure, and absenteeism. Classified employees into high, mid, low cohorts; calculated cost-per-cohort and absenteeism rate. Benchmarked against Visier and ChartHop, packaged into an 8-slide CHRO-style PowerPoint with executive summary, data visualizations and three prioritized HR recommendations. Delivered a board-ready people analytics report demonstrating C-suite-level data storytelling.

Excel Pivots Visier ChartHop PowerPoint
05

End-to-End eNPS Pulse Survey Cycle

Problem

No engagement baseline existed for a simulated organisation with no survey history.

Solution & Impact

Designed a 6-question eNPS survey in Culture Amp. Collected real responses from 20 professionals, calculated eNPS score, segmented by department and tenure. Built a 9-box performance-potential grid in Excel, drafted a formal survey policy document covering cadence and confidentiality. Built a 5-slide leadership insight deck in PowerPoint with engagement drivers and recommended HR interventions. Delivered a full live-data engagement cycle replicating exactly what an HR team executes post-survey.
